Qn6: MD5 what was the md5sum hash of the affected process

from the above question we can clearly see that the process ugxS.exe was the one so we need to get the PID (Process ID): We needed to dump the whole process to our server and get its hash

./volatility_2.6_lin64_standalone -f MemoryImage.mem --profile=Win7SP1x64 memdump --pid=1260 -D .

after getting the extracted process we head over to virus total to confirm if its malicious and to cross check other important details such as the day it was first submitted since our memory was only a day old wew had to get the exact day the memory was made

From virus Total we get matching results such as same day, same name of executable and md5sum hash of the malware We can also just
